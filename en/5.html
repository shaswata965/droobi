Droobi Health

Droobi is the only Arabic digital therapeutics product that uses behavior science to help you change your habits, improve your health and reduce the risk of chronic disease.

The droobi program focuses on diabetes management through exercise, healthy eating, adherence to medication, behavioral modification and the use of an evidence-based curriculum that aims to manage diabetes and control diabetes complications.

Weekly lessons include information about medication, nutrition, exercise, monitoring, adherence to medication, preventing, detecting, and treating acute and chronic complications, and overcoming the psychological barriers to lasting change.

Easy tracking- the program allows you to easily track your blood glucose, medication health, what you eat, drink, and do each day so your health coach can give real-time feedback and insight, while the droobi team understands your behavior.

Droobi will capture your data daily. You will enter your Blood Glucose with the help of your smart tracking gadgets. These gadgets will collect your data on your food portions, daily activity, and blood sugar levels.

Droobi will measure your engagement, weight loss, physical activities and will assess the effectiveness of the intervention and modify them as needed to improve the effectiveness of the intervention.
