Lesson 1
Program Details

This program will teach you how to monitor your blood sugar levels, eat healthy for life (and not just a quick diet fix), stay active on a daily basis, deal with stress, and cope with the challenges that you may face as you go through the program.

Remember, diabetes management is about preventing or reducing complications, so you can live a better quality life for you and those around you.

Complications are preventable if you start early and manage your diabetes well.
