The Components of Diabetes Care

The main goal for diabetes self-management is managing your blood sugar levels to reduce complications. The four main components of diabetes management are:

  • Meal management

    A guide for healthy eating

  • Physical activity

    A Physical Activity plan will help you maintain body weight, lower blood glucose, and decrease insulin resistance.

  • Monitoring

    Blood glucose records will help you and the healthcare team make changes to better manage your blood sugar.

  • Medication plan

    Taking medications as prescribed to help you control your blood sugar levels
