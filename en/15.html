How can Droobi change your habits?

Lifestyle change is based on replacing unhealthy behaviors with healthier ones. Droobi will help you improve and build healthy habits through:

  • Setting small-step individual goals

  • A customized diet and physical activity program, from detailed baseline questionnaire

  • Frequent reminders of the goals that you have chosen and the reasons why you have chosen them

  • Rich educational content on diabetes

  • Promotion of social support

  • Logging your diet, physical activity and glucose levels

  • Quizzes
