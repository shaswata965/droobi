Lesson 1
The Empowered Patient

To self-manage your disease, you need to be the leader of your healthcare team. You can manage your diabetes by:

  • Making regular and planned follow-up appointments with educators and clinicians

  • Recording your diet and physical activity, and monitoring your blood glucose levels

  • Describing your symptoms related to diabetes

  • Asking questions to your educators if you do not understand - discuss problems, concerns and record ideas/actions

  • Discussing behavior change goals with your health provider

  • Learning target values and record results

  • Understanding the benefits and risks of treatments, including medications

  • Understanding what the care team is saying; give medications and treatments a chance to work

  • Looking for opportunities for ongoing education and information

You have a number of specialists to help you with diabetes management. This includes regular management of blood glucose and medications, screening for complications such as eye, foot and dental disease, and lifestyle counselling.

Please ensure you take time out to visit your care team!
